# SOP-005 Incident Management

Document ID: SOP-005  
Version: 1.0  
Effective Date: 2026-04-06

## 1. Purpose

Define consistent triage, escalation, investigation, containment, and closure of incidents impacting compliance, security, or availability.

## 2. Incident Severity

- Sev-1: Critical impact to trial-critical operations or potential data integrity compromise.
- Sev-2: Major degradation without confirmed critical data loss.
- Sev-3: Minor issue with workaround.

## 3. Procedure

1. Detect and log incident with timestamp and reporter.
2. Classify severity and assign owner.
3. Contain impact and preserve evidence.
4. Perform root-cause analysis.
5. Execute CAPA and verify effectiveness.
6. Close with QA review and final report.

## 4. Notification Targets

- Sev-1 initial response: <= 30 minutes
- Sev-2 initial response: <= 4 hours
- Sev-3 initial response: <= 1 business day
